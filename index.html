Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

Driven by a commitment to bridging the digital divide

Our Vision

In today's digital age, access to information technology (IT) skills is crucial for personal and professional growth. However, many individuals in our community face barriers to acquiring these skills due to financial constraints. To address this issue, we propose a Free Community IT Training program, designed to provide underserved populations with the IT skills they need to thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by offering free IT training to individuals who lack access to these resources. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to develop the skills necessary to participate fully in the digital economy. Our program will provide training in essential IT skills, including computer basics, software applications, and online safety.

Community Initiative

Community IT Training Initiative

On July 11th, 2025, EbenGh Technologies organized a free IT training for over 180 pupils at Pantang Basic School. The hands-on training covered essential topics like Introduction to Computers, Social Media, and Graphic Design.

With 10 laptops provided for practical sessions, pupils gained valuable tech skills in a fun and interactive environment.
