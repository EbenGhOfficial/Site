Community IT Training Initiative

On July 11th, 2025, EbenGh Technologies organized a free IT training for over 180 pupils at Pantang Basic School. The hands-on training covered essential topics like Introduction to Computers, Social Media, and Graphic Design.

With 10 laptops provided for practical sessions, pupils gained valuable tech skills in a fun and interactive environment. This initiative by EbenGh Technologies bridged the digital gap for young learners, sparking interest in technology and creativity.

The training was a huge success, empowering the next generation with vital digital skills for the future.
